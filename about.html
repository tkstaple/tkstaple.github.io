At the University of California, Davis, I created my own individual major, called Behavioral Design with the ultimate goal to bridge the gap between design and psychology. I aim to benefit people’s lives by incorporating behavioral science and psychology principles into the design process, and moreover, to do so ethically.


Currently the lead designer at Foodfully, first author of a published design research study, recent graduate of UC Davis, and forever a student of life.


I aim to help people and the environment through the power of design.
