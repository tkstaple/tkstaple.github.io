I am fascinated by the relationship between psychology, technology, and design. This interdisciplinary interest ultimately culminated in 2015 when I created my own individual major at UC Davis, called Behavioral Design. The goal of the major was to “bridge the gap between design and psychology / behavioral science / cognitive science” to ultimately understand how to change people’s behavior to benefit their lives—and furthermore, to do so ethically.
