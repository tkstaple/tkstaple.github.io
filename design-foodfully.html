Foodfully App

Every year Americans throw out over 74 billion pounds of nutritious, tasty food—worth over $165 billion annually. With strong ties to the agricultural community and researchers at UC Davis, Foodfully's dedicated team of programmers, microbiologists, and designers is working hard to help you know when your food is at its freshest...and what to do with it.

Role

As Foodfully's Lead Designer, I manage a small team of designers, design the complete UI/UX of the Foodfully mobile app, along with brand identity, the website, pitchdecks, and more, and help pitch at competitions, such as TechCrunch Disrupt.

Brand Identity

The icon logo is a radish (dubbed "Ruby" at the moment) which was chosen as the food item for the brand identity due to two reasons: it represents food "freshness" well since is an iconic piece of produce, and it is the most similar in shape to an exclamation point. The latter is significant because of the app's main function to notify users for when their food is going bad. The dual-color/faux-3D style also emulates the duality of the app itself: between the Fridge and the Stove.
