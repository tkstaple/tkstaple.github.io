At the University of California, Davis, I created my own individual major, called Behavioral Design and my ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between psychology and design. My aim is to benefit people’s lives by incorporating behavioral science and psychology principles into the design process, and moreover, to do so ethically.


Currently the lead designer at Foodfully, P.I. of a design research study for bicycle safety, a part-time front-end web developer, recent graduate of UC Davis, and forever a student of life.


I aim to help people and the environment through the power of design.
