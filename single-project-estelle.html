Estelle — American Boy
Music Video

A half-minute ode to one of my favorite songs.

  • Motion Design

American Boy is an absolute classic — I wanted to emulate the feeling of traveling through the movement of iconic sites across the screen, taking the viewer on a trip of their own. Keeping the images black & white augments the eclectic vibe of the song, while also allowing the lyrics to stand out in the background.


Every image used in the video is royalty-free and was found via Librestock.com; none of the images were shot by me.


Made with Adobe AfterEffects & Photoshop CC.
